Ancient Places

With a history of expanding over 3000 years, Sri Lanka holds some of world's ancient cities including Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa and Digamadulla. Their once glorious townships, palaces, temples, monasteries, hospitals and theaters intricately carved and modeled out of stone lay and abandoned and forgotten with time amidst the soaring jungle.

Forests

Sri Lanka is a tropical country. The tropical forest - Sinharaja is world famous and a national heritage site. The forests in Sri Lanka are found both in the dry zone and wet zone. Forests are important because they help to maintain regular rainfall. They also provide wood for fuel purposes. Furthermore, forests are the natural habitats for wildlife and a variety of rare endemic flora and fauna.

Mountains

Sri Lanka is well known
all over the world for its
beautiful mountains and the
